Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

FlipKids: Learn English ("the App") is a children's educational app developed by Daniil ("we", "us", or "our"). We take your family's privacy seriously — especially when it comes to children.

No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos, videos, or microphone input

No Internet Connection

The App works entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet, does not make any network requests, and does not communicate with any servers.

No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Local Data Storage

The App stores game progress (level completion and star ratings) locally on your device using standard system storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when the App is deleted.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App is designed for children ages 3-7 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

No Ads or In-App Purchases

The App contains no advertisements and no in-app purchases.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since the App collects no data, we do not anticipate any changes.
